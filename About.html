Our History

Our Restaurant was founded by Chef Oscardo Leonel Banks. He started the as a small food shop along the road around from the year 1990-2006 then he later got enough capital to open this restaurant. The restaurant started effectively in the year 2008 and it had 8 chefs working round the clock but there wasn't a delivery service yet. Around the year 2013, Chef Oscardo came with the idea of a delivery service and it was an idea which took his restaurant to a 5 star review in the city. We have impaccable meals, a competent delivery service which delivers your well packaged meal at your confort of your home. We are noted for our dinner specials and our hospitable customer service which we offer to our customer.
